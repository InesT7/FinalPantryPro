Tomato Sauce Pasta
Ingredients
Tomatoes: About 4-5 medium-sized tomatoes, diced.
Onion: 1 medium-sized onion.
Cilantro (Coriander).
Jalapeño or Chili Pepper: 1-2.
Garlic: 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced.
Lime Juice: Juice of 1-2 limes.
Salt: To taste.
Black Pepper: To taste.
Instructions
1. Combine the diced tomatoes, chopped onion, cilantro, jalapeño, and garlic.
2. Add lime juice, salt, and black pepper to taste. Adjust the quantities.
3. If you're using optional ingredients like cumin or cayenne pepper, add them.
4. Mix all the ingredients together well.
5. Let the salsa sit for at least 15-30 minutes before serving.
6. Taste the salsa and adjust the seasoning.
